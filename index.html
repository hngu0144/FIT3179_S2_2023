Top Billionaires: Richest people in 2023

In March 2023, Forbes has collected the billionaires’s information such as stock and exchange rates from (Forbes, 2023). In this visualisation, we will have a quick look through the distribution of billionaires and country’s economy.

United State of America is at the lead of the billionaires list where they have 754 billionaires. Some famous face could be mentioned such as Bernard Arnault, Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, Warren Buffett or Bill Gates.

At the second place, China also produce multiple billionaires such as Zhong Shanshan, Jack Ma or Zhang Yiming (Flannery, 2022)

With the born of Artificial Inteligence and cutting edge technology, there is a group of people who work, invest in technology field (Liu, 2023).

Based on the graph, a majority of people that born between 1950 to 1970 were produce the most billionaires. These fall into the categories: Technology, Manufacturing, Finance & Investment

Comparing the gender, the total wealth from male is visually higher than female. Male billionaires has the domain of age sqread from 18 to 101 while female billionaires has the domain of age sqread from 35 to 101

However, at some certain age, the number of female billionaires are higher than male billionaires, for example, at age 64 or 73.

Self-made billionaires are made the business by themselves while inherited billionaires inherited from their close relationship, usually family.

Some famous Self-made billionaires worth to mention are Jeff Bezos and Mark Zuckerberg (Livemint, 2023)

GDP stand for Gross Domestic Product, which is one of the important measurement for a country. It is the total value of every product of a country has (Fernando, 2023)

CPI is another key metric, where it measures the inflation and price change of household (Australian Bureau of Statistic, 2023).

Life expentancy is another good measurement of a country. It measures the average life-span for each country (Max Roser, Esteban Ortiz-Ospina and Hannah Ritchie, 2019)

Japan is famous about their high life expentancy since they have a healthy life which lead to low rate of being sick or getting cancer (Juneau, 2021)

As the definition from an institution of statistic, Gross Enrolment Ratio would measure the probability of number of enrolment to a certain education level (Unesco, 2023). In the chart, we would see the actual number of enrolment and illustrate the mean of them.

Nepal has the highest average primary education enrolment while Greece has the highest average tertiary education enrolment
